Welcome to Sindhu Manthan Foundation

Sindhu Manthan Foundation was found in 9th November 2022, as a Charitable Public Trust by a group of diverse individuals and professionals with the aim of promoting community engagement and leadership with specific reference to the fields of health, human development, gender equality, and human rights. In this connection, the organization adopted a participatory approach to engage the marginalized and under privilege community to highlight their issues and concerns for equitable development,
